# Company Policy Document

## Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting user privacy and data security.

### Data Collection

We collect only necessary information for service delivery.

### Data Usage

User data is used solely for providing and improving our services.

## Security Commitment

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect all data.

### Access Control

Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

### Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

## Compliance

We comply with all applicable regulations including GDPR and CCPA.
